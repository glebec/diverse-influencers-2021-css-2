Penguins

Penguins live in Antarctica and are famous for marching miles to their breeding and nesting grounds. Male penguins balance the egg on their feet while femal penguins return to the sea to fish for food. Baby penguins are very cute and fuzzy.

Manatees

Manatees are very slow, gentle, and fat mammals. They are herbivores and are vulnerable to boat strikes. Baby manatees are called calves.
